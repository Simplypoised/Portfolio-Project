The Best Movies According to Sylvia

My top 3 movies of all-time

The Sting

"The Sting" is a 1973 American film set in September 1936. It is about two professional grifters as they attempt to swindle a mob boss

Equalizer

Denzel Washinton is one of my favorite actors. This movie has sequels in which Denzel comes to the aid of strangers.

The Five Heartbeats

The Five Heartbeats is a movie based on The Dells, a music group. The movie detailed the life and works of the group.
